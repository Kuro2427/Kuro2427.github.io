Dictionarium Lutris

Ordo post insaniam. est. 2024

Welcome to Dictionarium Lutris. This project aims to compile and define the often nonsensical terms used by me and my friends.

It is a semi-open source project. Submissions are sent in via the appropriate thread on the server's forum, and curated and formatted by me. Please enjoy your stay.

Scrungle /ˈskrʌŋɡəl/

  1. [Adjective] Something that looks broken or otherwise messed up, typically referring to computer graphics, art or animals.
  2. [Verb] To mess up, or rather, scrungle something.

"Wow, look at the scrungled ass topology on that model"

"Don't set the texture compression to poor, that scrungles them"

You look like you would /juː lʊk laɪk juː wʊd/

  1. [Interjection] A quote from The Angry Birds Movie, typically used lightheartedly to poke fun at someone for saying or doing something that matches their perceived personality or customs.

"Guys, I just bought another old computer"
"Yeah you look like you would "

Yeah I'm a good editor /jeə aɪm ə gʊd ˈedɪtə/

  1. [Interjection] A phrase commonly used to criticize a creative artist/work in reference to it being horridly bad or unpolished based off of an ironic gif. Editor can be used interchangeably for any other type of work or art form. Shortened from “yeah I am a good editor you can hire me for 50$ a minute.”

“Hey look at this messy artwork/editing/modeling”
“yeah I’m a good artist/editor/modeler you can hire me at 50$ a minute”

Is this you /ɪz ðɪs jʊ/

  1. [Phrase] Commonly used to lightheartedly point out something related to a person. Typically character or animal that they relate to or are known to like.
    Other common variations include “Is this what you look like (/ɪz ðɪs wɒt jʊ lʊk laɪk/)” and “This is what you look like (/ðɪs ɪz wɒt jʊ lʊk laɪk/)”

“Hey Lily, is this what you look like”
“Yeah that’s meeeee :3”
