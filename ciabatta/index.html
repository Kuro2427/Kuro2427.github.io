Ciabatta clicker!

Welcome to Ciabatta clicker!
Ciabatta clicker is a Cookie clicker inspired shitpost idle game where you collect bread by clicking bread to buy more bread.

Before you begin playing, I just wanted to give some warnings and heads-ups about this game:

- The current build is broken. Like, "You can't save your progress because that hasn't been programmed in yet" broken. This game was made in one weekend lol.

- You can technically play on a phone if you tap the "view desktop website" option but it won't be a plesant experience.

- I'll probably update this game to fix issues and add more content but honestly don't expect much. It's still a joke game made out of spite.

- There's an engine glitch that blasts random sound effects whenever you tab back into the game, so if you don't want to be deafened please turn down your browser's volume.

Thank you for reading this far. Have fun! :D
