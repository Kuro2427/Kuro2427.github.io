Amidst your other birthday presents, you found something you hadn't seen before...

It's a card, and a very obviously homemade one at that. Hastily scribbled in crayon on the worn down red paper, above a... Crying yellow star?? It reads "Merry Xmas! BIRTH." Seems like whoever made you this card must have repurposed one they didn't get to gift you on Christmas.

Perhaps out of morbid curiosity, you decide to open it. In the worst handwriting you've ever seen, it reads:

every day, a million babies are born in the united states alone and today twenty one years ago that baby is YOU i hope you have a very nice birth day and very your wishes come true there is also "gift"

A small piece of paper fell from the card as you opened it. Upon closer inspection, it seems to be...

A coupon for 20% off a large pizza... From a chain you're pretty sure is from another country...

You suddenly get the urge to dispose of this card in an appropriate manner.

BURN BABY BURN
